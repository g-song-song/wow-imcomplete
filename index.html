Now, I am complete is a World of Warcraft addon. With this addon, you can shout like Illidan Stormrage. See demos below:

Locale: English (Default)

Currently, there are 4 shouting phrases in English and one of them are randomly chosen:
  • Now, I am complete!
  • Feel the hatred of ten thousand years!
  • You are not prepared!
  • Now, you are prepared!

Locale: Korean

Currently, there are 5 shouting phrases in Korean (one is related with a meme in Korea) and one of them are randomly chosen:
  • 이제 난 완전해졌다!
  • 만 년 동안 응어리진 증오를 보여주마!
  • 너흰 아직 준비가 안 됐다!
  • 이제 너희는 준비가 돼-따!
  • 이제 난 완전히 졌다!
